Basic Lab test
BMP

A basic metabolic panel (BMP) is a helpful and common test that measures several important aspects of your blood.
Healthcare providers often use it as a go-to blood test to assess your general physical health, and it can also help diagnose, screen for and monitor certain health conditions.

This test combines the Basic Metabolic Panel with six more tests for a more comprehensive evaluation of metabolic functions, with a focus on organ systems.A comprehensive metabolic panel (CMP) is a test that measures 14 different substances in your blood. It provides important information about your body's chemical balance and metabolism. Metabolism is the process of how the body uses food and energy.
